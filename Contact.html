About Us

Young & Beautiful Salon-Spa has been in the practice of helping people feel young, beautiful and relaxed for over 20 years with 3 salon-spa locations. Located in the heart of Mississauga on Dundas Street, we have served thousands of satisfied loyal clients of various ethnicities. Our Mission at Young & Beautiful Salon-Spa is for every customer that comes into our salon for a service to leave with a younger and more beautiful self, a big smile, and a new positive life outlook. Our priorities are to provide our clients with professional and safe treatments in a comfortable and sanitized environment, and a team of certified beauty experts in our extensive selections of service: Nails, Hair, Facials, Waxing, Professional Makeup, Permanent Makeup and Eyelash Extensions. With the grand opening of our new beautiful and bigger facility, we strive to bring you a new salon-spa experience that is worth every dollar spent. We are grateful for the support of the community and our loyal customers, and we look forward to serving both existing and new clients for many years to come. Come to Young and Beautiful Salon-Spa and stay young and beautiful forever!
