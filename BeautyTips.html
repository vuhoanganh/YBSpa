WHAT ARE EYELASH EXTENSIONS?

They are single strands of synthetic eyelashes, available in many fiber types such as silk or mink, that are curved to replicate a natural lash. They are glued to your upper line so they look natural and part of your real lashes.

It’s a popular hybrids gel/polish manicure that’s cured with UV or LED light to harden the polish. How is Shellac different from fake nails? Fake nails require a drill for application while Shellac is much more gentle on natural nails.
